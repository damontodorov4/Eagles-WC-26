How it works
01
Pay your $50
Bank transfer to the comp organiser before submitting tips. Entry isn't locked until payment is confirmed.
02
Submit your tips
Pick your result for each match and predict the exact score. Tips must be in before each round deadline.
03
Score points
1 point for getting the result right. 1 bonus point if your exact scoreline is correct too.
04
Win the pot
Most points at the end wins 50% of the total pot. Tiebreaker: total goals tipped vs total goals scored.
